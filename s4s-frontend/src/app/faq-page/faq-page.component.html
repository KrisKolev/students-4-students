Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is this website about?


A: This website was inspired by already existing services, but with added features. It is intended to be used mostly by students, hence the name. In here only students can register and submit comments or upload locations.


Q: How can I create an account?


A: Creating an account is a very simple step. Click on the "Sign in" button in the top right corner and then click "Create an account". Once you've done that, fill in the fields and use your STUDENT email to register.


Q: Can I still use the website if I am not a student?


A: Yes. Even if you are not a student, you can still use the website to find some of the best restaurants or places to visit. But you will not be able to create an account or rate any existing locations.


Q: How can I know if my University is recognised?


A: We have ensured that EVERY university in the world is recognised by our website. Just use your student email when registering and logging in.
